Methodology & sources

Why you can trust this number

  • Statutory rate is Bank of England base rate plus 8 percentage points, per the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 (as amended by the 2002 Regulations).
  • BoE base rate is held at 5.25% in this teaser (2026 hold value); the platform version pulls the live rate from the BoE feed.
  • Fixed compensation tiers come from S5A introduced by the 2013 Late Payment Regulations: £40 for debts < £1,000, £70 for £1,000-£9,999, £100 for £10,000+.
  • For multi-period interest where the base rate changed mid-overdue, use the full CrowCash engine.
