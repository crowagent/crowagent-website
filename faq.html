General

Foundation, signup, and getting started.

What is CrowAgent?
CrowAgent is a compliance software platform with four products: CrowMark, the UK bid suite, CrowCyber for Cyber Essentials v3.3 Danzell, CrowCash for late-payment recovery under the Late Payment Act 1998, and CrowESG for VSME ESG reporting and carbon footprints. A free CSRD Applicability Checker tests post-Omnibus I scope in seconds. Every product turns the source regulation into a self-serve compliance output in minutes, not weeks.
How do I sign up?
Visit app.crowagent.ai/signup to create your account. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can start scoring bids or preparing your evidence immediately after signing up.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. Every paid plan includes a 14-day free trial. You get full access to all features during the trial period. No credit card is required to start. The CSRD Checker is always free with no account needed.

Products

Detailed guidance on PPN 002, Cyber Essentials, and our compliance engines.

What is Cyber Essentials v3.3 (Danzell)?
Cyber Essentials is the UK government-backed scheme, run by the NCSC and IASME, that certifies an organisation against five technical controls. The v3.3 (Danzell) question set comes into force on 27 April 2026 and adds requirements such as mandatory multi-factor authentication on cloud admin accounts. CrowCyber is a preparation and evidence tool that maps your posture to every control and flags the Danzell auto-fail conditions; it is not a certification body.
What is PPN 002?
PPN 002 (Procurement Policy Note 002) is UK government policy dated February 2025 and mandatory from 1 October 2025. It requires a minimum 10% social value weighting on in-scope central government contract evaluations, and sets out five missions, M1 to M5, with eight policy outcomes that suppliers evidence in their bids.
What is the 10% minimum?
Under PPN 002, at least 10% of the total evaluation score for in-scope central government contracts must be allocated to social value. Many local authorities and NHS trusts apply the same or a higher weighting. CrowMark lets you model the weighting your buyer published, calculates the social-value total in code, and drafts the narrative with a PPN 017 AI-transparency disclosure for you to approve.
How is social value scored?
CrowMark calculates it deterministically in code, using unit-aware arithmetic over a curated catalogue of 19 social-value measures that are aligned to National TOMs conventions and mapped to the 2025 PPN 002 model of five missions and eight policy outcomes. It is not a full National TOMs implementation. The language model never computes the figure: any generated prose containing a £ or % that the computed figures do not support is rejected rather than shown.
Is CrowMark ready to use?
CrowMark is live and in daily use, with more being added. Tender discovery from Contracts Finder and Find a Tender, document ingestion, grounded answer drafting, deterministic PPN 002 calculation, delivery tracking and Procurement Act 2023 s.52 and s.71 KPI checks all work today. See the CrowMark page for what each stage covers.

Pricing

Plans, billing, and subscription management.

What plans are available?
CrowMark, CrowCyber, CrowCash, and CrowESG each have their own plan tiers, from Free through Starter, Pro, and Portfolio. All paid plans include a 14-day free trial and annual billing saves 10%. See current pricing at /pricing.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings > Billing in the platform. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. Your data is preserved, so if you resubscribe later, everything will still be there.
How do billing periods work?
Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually (10% discount). Your billing period starts from the date you subscribe. After your 14-day trial, you will be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't subscribe, your data is preserved but you won't be able to create new records or contracts.

Security

Data integrity, encryption, and privacy standards.

What data do you use?
Social value is computed in code from the PPN 002 (February 2025) model with unit-aware arithmetic over CrowMark's curated measure catalogue. Cyber Essentials logic follows the NCSC v3.3 (Danzell) controls. Carbon figures use the DEFRA/DESNZ conversion factors, and statutory interest uses the Bank of England base rate under the Late Payment Act 1998. Every figure traces to its named source.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security on all tables. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We are protected by Cloudflare (DDoS, Bot Fight Mode, SSL Full Strict). Authentication uses Supabase Auth with optional TOTP MFA. CrowAgent Ltd is registered with the ICO as a data controller and we are GDPR compliant. See our Security page for full details.
