Pricing FAQ

Is there a free tier?
The CSRD Checker is free and does not require an account. CrowAgent Core, CrowMark, CrowCyber, and CrowCash are paid products with a 14-day free trial.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can move to a different plan from billing once your account is live. Your data persists across plan changes.
How does annual billing work?
Pay upfront for the year and save 10% compared to monthly billing. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time inside the platform.
What happens when my trial ends?
At the end of the 14-day trial you can choose whether to continue on a paid plan. You are not charged during the trial. Your data is preserved if you decide to subscribe later.
Do you offer enterprise pricing?
Yes. Enterprise plans start at £999/month and include SSO, custom SLA, dedicated customer success, and bespoke integrations. Contact sales@crowagent.ai for a quote.
Is the multi-product bundle discount stackable?
Yes. The 15% multi-product discount stacks with the 10% annual saving. Combine any two paid products and bill annually for the full 25% effective saving.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel from Settings > Billing in the platform. Access continues until the end of the billing period. Data is preserved.
Is my data secure?
AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. UK data residency. ICO registered data controller. GDPR compliant. SOC 2 in progress.
