# CrowAgent Product Winability Research — Homepage Strategy
**Date:** 2026-05-23
**Goal:** Identify highest-winability product for UK SME market + transform homepage to lead with it.

## Product portfolio analysis

### Tier 1 — HIGHEST WINABILITY (lead the homepage with these)

#### 🏆 #1 **CrowCash** — Late Payment Recovery
- **Audience size:** ALL UK SMEs (5.5M businesses)
- **Pain intensity:** EXTREME — UK SMEs owed **£26 billion** in unpaid invoices (FSB data)
- **Statutory entitlement:** Bank of England base rate + 8% statutory interest under Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 — often unclaimed
- **Buying signal:** instant — "I have invoices over 30 days late"
- **ROI proof:** one £5,000 invoice recovered with statutory interest = £400+ recovered = pays for the platform
- **Stripe-grade value prop:** "Your invoices are late. You're owed BoE+8% in statutory interest. Click to recover with one letter."
- **No regulatory deadline needed** — universal SME problem
- **Sales motion:** PLG (product-led growth) — single SME signs up, recovers first invoice, expands

#### 🏆 #2 **CrowCyber** — Cyber Essentials v3.3 Danzell
- **Audience size:** Every UK SME bidding for public sector work (~500k+ businesses)
- **Pain intensity:** HARD DEADLINE — 28 April 2026 came into force (NCSC v3.3 Danzell)
- **Regulatory mandate:** PPN 014 requires Cyber Essentials for most government contracts
- **Buying signal:** "I have a tender deadline AND no Cyber Essentials cert"
- **ROI proof:** missing certification = disqualified from £20bn+ public procurement
- **Stripe-grade value prop:** "Your licence to bid for UK government contracts. Pass Cyber Essentials v3.3 in 14 days."
- **Hard regulatory teeth** — buy or die
- **Sales motion:** time-bound urgency

#### 🏆 #3 **CrowMark** — Social Value Bid Scoring (PPN 002)
- **Audience size:** UK SMEs bidding for central government contracts (~50-100k businesses)
- **Pain intensity:** HIGH — losing tenders by 2-5 points because social value weighting is 10% minimum
- **Regulatory mandate:** PPN 002 (Cabinet Office, Feb 2025) mandates 10% social value weighting
- **Buying signal:** "I just lost a tender on social value scoring"
- **ROI proof:** one £500k contract won = 50-100× ROI
- **Stripe-grade value prop:** "Win 10% more public-sector bids with verified Social Value scores tied to Oxford TOMs."
- **High-value but narrower audience**
- **Sales motion:** ABM (account-based) — target bid teams

### Tier 2 — SECONDARY (future-proofing teaser, not the lead)

#### CrowAgent Core — MEES Band C 2028 (proposed)
- **Audience size:** Commercial property owners + REITs (~250k UK properties)
- **Pain intensity:** HIGH but FUTURE — £150k max fine per property under SI 2015/962 reg 38
- **Issue:** Band C 2028 is **PROPOSED**, not enacted — softer urgency
- **Buying signal:** "I own commercial property and need to know my exposure"
- **Sales motion:** discovery → portfolio scan → priced retrofit pathway

#### CrowESG — Multi-framework ESG (Q3 2026)
- **Audience size:** Large UK groups (1,000+ employees per Omnibus I threshold)
- **Pain intensity:** MEDIUM — framework consolidation post-CSRD Omnibus I
- **Status:** Not yet launched — waitlist only
- **Sales motion:** early-access waitlist

#### CSRD Checker — Free Omnibus I applicability
- **Audience size:** All UK businesses checking CSRD scope
- **Pain intensity:** LOW (it's a check, not a product)
- **Use case:** **FREE LEAD MAGNET** — gates the funnel
- **Strategy:** put as a prominent "Free tool" at top of funnel

## Customer persona analysis

### Persona A — "The Cash-Strapped SME Owner"
- **Profile:** UK SME, 10-50 employees, B2B services or trades
- **Pain:** 40% of invoices paid 60+ days late
- **Primary product fit:** **CrowCash**
- **Hook:** "You're owed £X,000 in late-payment interest you haven't claimed."
- **Conversion path:** Free invoice late-payment calculator → upload invoices → CrowCash subscription

### Persona B — "The Government Contractor / Bid Manager"
- **Profile:** UK SME bidding for council, NHS, MoD contracts
- **Pain:** Lost £500k contract on Social Value scoring + needs Cyber Essentials cert
- **Primary product fit:** **CrowCyber + CrowMark bundle**
- **Hook:** "Your licence to bid: Cyber Essentials. Your edge to win: 10% Social Value."
- **Conversion path:** PPN 002 calculator (free) → CrowMark + CrowCyber subscription

### Persona C — "The Property Compliance Officer"
- **Profile:** REIT, commercial landlord, property manager
- **Pain:** MEES Band E enforcement now, Band C 2028 looming
- **Primary product fit:** **CrowAgent Core**
- **Hook:** "£150k per non-compliant property. Scan your portfolio in 90 seconds."
- **Conversion path:** MEES risk snapshot (free) → portfolio CrowAgent Core subscription

## Homepage transformation strategy

**Current state issues:**
- 17 sections (too many — Stripe has 7-8)
- 17,989px tall (Stripe ~6,500px)
- Sparse layouts, weak motion
- Lead pillar unclear (currently treats all 3 Revenue Trinity products equally)

**Strategic changes:**
1. **Lead with CrowCash** — biggest universal pain, instant ROI
2. **Reorder Revenue Trinity:** CrowCash → CrowCyber → CrowMark (current is CrowMark → CrowCyber → CrowCash)
3. **Make CrowCash card slightly more prominent** — featured-card style
4. **Add Late Payment Calculator** as the inline lead magnet (currently MEES postcode checker is the inline tool — switch to Late Payment since that's the higher-converting funnel)
5. **Compress section count** from 17 to ~10
6. **Add premium motion** — scroll-triggered fade-ups, parallax depth, hover lifts (Stripe-grade)

- CTA click-through on free tools >5%
- Trial conversion from CrowCash hook >2%
