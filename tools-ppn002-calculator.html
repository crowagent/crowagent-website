Methodology in one paragraph

What the calculator does

The calculator takes contract value, sector, and duration. It applies the PPN 002 minimum 10% social value weighting, suggests TOMs measures with Oxford Social Value Bank 2023–24 proxy values, and shows the implied social value floor in pounds. Sources are cited inline. The full CrowMark platform extends this to narrative generation, evidence tracking, and PDF export.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the minimum weighting?

PPN 002 (effective 24 February 2025) sets the minimum social value weighting at 10%.

Does this replace PPN 06/20?

PPN 002 replaces PPN 06/20 for new procurements from 24 February 2025. Contracts already procured under PPN 06/20 continue under that framework.

Do I need an account?

No. The calculator is free. The full CrowMark platform is a paid product with TOMs scoring, narrative generation, and evidence tracking.
