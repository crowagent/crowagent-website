How it is calculated

Every figure traces back to the statute.

Statutory interest and fixed compensation are set by the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 and the rate set by SI 2002/1674. This applies to B2B and business-to-public-sector commercial debts only.

Statutory interest
8% above the Bank of England base rate, applied to the principal across the days the invoice is overdue. Interest continues to accrue daily until the debt is paid.
1998 Act, s.6
SI 2002/1674
Fixed compensation
A fixed sum per overdue invoice: £40 for a debt under £1,000, £70 for £1,000 to £9,999.99, and £100 for £10,000 or more.
1998 Act, s.5A
