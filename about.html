CrowAgent translates complex UK statutes into deterministic business intelligence. We build the software that helps UK SMEs compete with incumbent consultancies across cyber, procurement, property, and ESG.

The approach

Every feature starts with the actual regulation: SI 2015/962 for energy efficiency, PPN 002 for social value, EU 2022/2464 for sustainability reporting. The source legislation drives the workflow step by step, so you get answers you can trust.

What you get

Tools that read the actual regulations: MEES penalty schedules (SI 2015/962), Cyber Essentials v3.3 Danzell controls, the Late Payment Act 1998 statutory interest formula, PPN 002 social value models, the post-Omnibus I CSRD thresholds, and the EFRAG VSME 2024 SME standard. You get clear, defensible workflows, not guesswork, not generic templates.
