How the checker works

From two numbers to defensible scope in three steps

Take the threshold inputs

Average employees and net turnover at year-end (group basis), plus a single growth flag. Two numbers and one yes/no question is the entire interface.

Apply the post-Omnibus I test

Both >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover are required. The checker runs the AND test and the forward-risk layer for companies trending into scope within two reporting years.

Render scope + citations

In-scope / out-of-scope / forward-risk verdict, the gap to threshold (when below), and Directive (EU) 2026/470 recital citations. Audit committee ready.

What changed under Omnibus I?

Both >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover are now required (not either-or).

Are old thresholds still relevant?

No. The old 250-employee / €40M turnover thresholds are superseded.
