Methodology in one paragraph

What the calculator does

The calculator takes invoice value, due date, and payment date. It applies the prevailing Bank of England base rate plus 8 percentage points across the elapsed period and adds the fixed-sum compensation tier (£40 / £70 / £100). Output is a one-page itemised demand letter you can send. The full CrowCash platform extends this to chase sequences, debtor ageing, and AI payment-likelihood scoring.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the statutory rate?

Bank of England base rate plus 8 percentage points, applied from the due date until the debt is paid in full.

What are the £40 / £70 / £100 tiers?

Fixed-sum compensation: £40 for debts under £1,000, £70 for £1,000–£9,999.99, and £100 for £10,000 and over.

Do I have to charge it?

It is a statutory entitlement, not contractual. You can choose to charge or waive.
