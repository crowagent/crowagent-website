Methodology & Sources

We base this calculator on the latest EU legislative updates to ensure your scope assessment is accurate and defensible.

Omnibus I Thresholds

Both thresholds are required: >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover, per Directive (EU) 2026/470 (Omnibus I, in force 18 March 2026). The old 250-employee / €40M floor is superseded and is not used by this calculator.

Forward Risk Logic

Forward-risk status is triggered when you self-report a likelihood of crossing both thresholds within 2 reporting years. This follows the ESRS recommendation for early preparation.

Group Consolidation

Final scope depends on entity-level facts, parent vs subsidiary, and listed-entity status. Non-EU groups with EU branches are also subject to specific €150M local turnover rules.

Value Chain Disclosure

Entities falling outside the mandatory scope may still face reporting pressure through Layer 3 value-chain exposure. The full CSRD Checker product covers these requirements.
