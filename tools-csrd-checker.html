Methodology in one paragraph

What the checker does

CSRD Omnibus I narrowed in-scope companies to those with both more than 1,000 employees AND more than €450M turnover. The checker runs the threshold test and a forward-look risk layer (group consolidation, parent-undertaking exemptions). It does not replace legal advice but gives a defensible first pass for board packs and audit preparation.

FAQ

Common questions

What changed under Omnibus I?

Both >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover are now required (not either-or).

Are old thresholds still relevant?

No. The old 250-employee / €40M turnover thresholds are superseded.

What if I'm below the threshold?

EFRAG VSME (2024) is the SME-friendly path. Use our VSME Materiality Light tool.
