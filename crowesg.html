Platform features

Multi-framework
Orchestration.

Six core capabilities designed to eliminate redundant ESG reporting effort across your entire group.

5 frameworks in one

GRI Standards 2021, TCFD, CSRD/ESRS (post-Omnibus I), ISSB IFRS S1 & S2, and UK SDR. Pre-mapped disclosure libraries kept current as standard-setters ship updates.

Double materiality

Stakeholder input matrix combining internal scoring with external surveys. Outputs a defensible materiality matrix showing impact and financial materiality side-by-side.

Metric mapping

Define each KPI once in CrowESG and the platform routes it to every relevant disclosure. Edit the source figure once, every linked report and disclosure updates automatically.

Audit evidence

Every metric, narrative, and reclassification is timestamped and linked to source documents. Versioned snapshots provide the trail limited and reasonable assurance assignments require.

Restatements

Methodology changes and prior-year restatements are tracked with full provenance. Year-on-year comparability is built in. Assurers see exactly what changed and the justification for the change.

Multi-output

Generate XHTML/iXBRL for ESRS digital tagging, board-ready PDF reports, CSV/Excel data extracts, and a draft sustainability statement. All from the same underlying metric set.
