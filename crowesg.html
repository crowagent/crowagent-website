Why CrowESG

Three reasons sustainability teams choose CrowESG

Define once, report everywhere

Cross-framework metric mapping means a single Scope 1 emissions number satisfies GRI 305, ESRS E1, ISSB IFRS S2, TCFD Metrics & Targets, and UK SDR, without rewriting it five times.

Double materiality, done properly

Stakeholder input matrix runs both impact materiality (your effects on people and planet) and financial materiality (sustainability effects on your business), the methodology ESRS expects and assurers accept.

Audit-ready evidence trail

Every metric, every narrative, every reclassification is timestamped and linked to source documents. Hand the audit pack to your assurer instead of building it the week before deadline.

Platform features

Every framework your group needs

5 frameworks in one platform

GRI Standards 2021, TCFD, CSRD/ESRS (post-Omnibus I), ISSB IFRS S1 & S2, and UK SDR. Pre-mapped disclosure libraries kept current as each standard-setter publishes updates.

Double materiality assessment

Stakeholder input matrix combines internal scoring with external surveys. Outputs a defensible materiality matrix showing impact materiality and financial materiality side-by-side.

Cross-framework metric mapping

Define each KPI once in CrowESG and the platform routes it to every relevant disclosure across the 5 frameworks. Edit the source figure once, every report updates.

Audit-ready evidence trail

Every metric is timestamped and linked to its source document. Restatements, methodology changes, and approvals are version-controlled, exactly what limited and reasonable assurance assignments require.

Restatements & year-on-year tracking

Methodology changes and prior-year restatements are tracked with full provenance. Year-on-year comparability is built in. Assurers see exactly what changed and why.

Multi-output exports

Generate XHTML/iXBRL for ESRS digital tagging, board-ready PDF reports, CSV/Excel data extracts, and a draft sustainability statement. All from the same metric set.
