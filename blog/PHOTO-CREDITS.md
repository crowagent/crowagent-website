## Editing notes

- `ppn-002-social-value-guide.jpg` is a landscape crop (1600 x 1000) taken from the centre of the
  original portrait frame. No other content changes were made to any image.
- All images were resized to 1600px wide and re-encoded (JPEG quality 82 with mozjpeg, WebP quality 76
  to 82) so that every file stays under 300KB.
- The same photograph is reused for an article's own hero and for the related-article cards that point
  at that article, so each article is represented by exactly one photograph.
