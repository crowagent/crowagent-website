## 🎭 The Narrative Architecture

### 1. The Silence (Prologue)
We begin with absolute "silence"—extreme whitespace and a single point of light. This creates **visual tension**. We are not shouting; we are announcing a standard. The typography is light (`Plus Jakarta Sans` at 200 weight), forcing the user to lean in and focus.

### 2. The Awakening (Hero)
As you scroll, the "Engine" wakes up. The hero section uses **extreme typographic scale** (10-12vw) to establish dominance. We use `WIN. PROTECT. ENFORCE.` as a cinematic mantra.

### 3. The Sovereign Spine
Instead of standard cards, these are **3D Statute Monoliths**. They use `perspective-3d` and `rotateY` transitions on scroll, making the regulatory pillars feel physically present and immovable.

### 4. The Engine (Immersive Shift)
This is the "Surprise Moment." As you scroll through the "Engine" section, the camera (viewport) physically "dives" into the CrowAgent core. The SVG CPU icon scales by **5,000%**, acting as a transition portal into the "Truth" layer. This creates a powerful emotional sense of "going under the hood."

### 5. Intentional Pacing
- **Visual Rhythm:** We alternate between high-density technical cards and massive, singular typographic statements.
- **Micro-Precision:** Button interactions use 700ms transitions to feel "heavy" and "expensive."
- **Layering:** A subtle 4% noise overlay and `backdrop-blur: 3xl` create a sense of high-end materiality.

## 💎 Elite Engineering Details
- **Motion Physics:** GSAP timelines with `expo.out` easing for that "magnetic" feel.
- **Scroll Hijacking (Restrained):** Lenis provides smooth, buttery momentum without breaking native browser expectations.
- **Hardware Acceleration:** All large transformations use `scale` and `opacity` to maintain 60fps on retina displays.
- **Statute-Cited:** Every "Statute Card" cites real UK legislation (SI 2015/962, Late Payment Act 1998) to anchor the cinematic visuals in business reality.
