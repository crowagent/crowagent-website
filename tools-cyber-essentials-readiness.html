Methodology in one paragraph

What the readiness check does

The readiness check covers the five Cyber Essentials technical themes (firewalls, secure configuration, user access, malware protection, security update management). It scores each on a traffic-light scale and flags any answer that would constitute a v3.3 auto-fail. Output is a one-page summary you can share with IT or your Certifying Body.

FAQ

Common questions

What changed in v3.3?

Cyber Essentials v3.3 ('Danzell'), effective 27 April 2026, makes MFA gaps and 14-day patch SLA breaches auto-fails.

Does PPN 014 require Cyber Essentials?

PPN 014 mandates Cyber Essentials for in-scope government, NHS, and MOD suppliers.

Is this certification?

No. The readiness check is a self-test. Certification goes through an IASME-licensed Certifying Body.
