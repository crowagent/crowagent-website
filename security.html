SECURITY & TRUST

Enterprise-grade security from day one

CrowAgent is built on UK infrastructure with data protection, encryption, and access controls designed for regulated industry use.

AES-256 encryption

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. Encryption keys are managed at the infrastructure level and rotated automatically.

UK & EU data residency

Primary customer data is stored in Supabase’s UK region. Supporting services may process metadata in the EU (PostHog EU Cloud) or under Standard Contractual Clauses (Sentry, Resend). No personal data is sent to AI providers for training. See our Privacy Policy for the full sub-processor list.

GDPR compliant

CrowAgent Ltd is registered as a data controller with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) under the Data Protection (Charges and Information) Regulations 2018. Verifiable on the ICO public register by searching for “CrowAgent Ltd”. We maintain a full Data Processing Agreement (DPA). Rights supported: right to erasure, data portability, restriction of processing, and access. Contact hello@crowagent.ai for data requests.

Role-based access control

Platform access is controlled by organisation-scoped roles (Owner, Admin, Member). Row-Level Security (RLS) is enforced at the database level - users can only access data belonging to their organisation. MFA is available for all accounts.

ISO 27001 aligned

Our security controls programme is aligned with ISO 27001 principles. We plan to pursue formal certification as the business scales. We conduct regular internal security reviews and apply OWASP best practices to all application development.

AI model data handling

AI inference (narrative generation, compliance analysis) uses Google Gemini via server-side API calls only. No customer data is sent to AI providers for training. AI inputs and outputs are not retained by model providers under our enterprise terms. All AI processing occurs on CrowAgent's infrastructure.

Vulnerability disclosure

Report security vulnerabilities responsibly to security@crowagent.ai with the subject ‘Security Disclosure’. We acknowledge within 2 business days. Resolution targets by severity: Critical (RCE, auth bypass, data exfiltration) within 5 days; High (privilege escalation, sensitive-data exposure) within 14 days; Medium within 30 days; Low / informational on a best-effort basis.

Uptime commitment

CrowAgent targets 99.5% monthly uptime. Independent monitoring is live at status.crowagent.ai. For security disclosures contact security@crowagent.ai; for general support use support@crowagent.ai.
