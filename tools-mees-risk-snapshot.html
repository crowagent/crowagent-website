Methodology in one paragraph

What the snapshot does

The snapshot looks up your current EPC band on the MHCLG Open Data Communities register, applies the rateable-value penalty formula in SI 2015/962 reg 39 (10% short-breach, 20% long-breach), and caps the result at £150,000. It also flags whether your property would meet the Band C 2028 proposed target if that target became law. Sources are cited inline; outputs are shareable.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the maximum MEES fine?

Maximum fine is rateable-value-based, capped at £150,000 per SI 2015/962 reg 39.

Is Band C 2028 current law?

Band C 2028 is a proposed deadline, not yet confirmed law. MEES Band E (Energy Efficiency Regulations 2015) has applied since 2018 for new lettings and since April 2023 for continuing tenancies.

Do I need an account?

No. The snapshot runs in your browser. You only create an account if you upgrade to the full CrowAgent Core platform for portfolio management and PDF reports.
