C

CSRD

CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)

EU directive requiring large companies to report on sustainability. Omnibus I raised thresholds to >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover.

E

EPC

EPC (Energy Performance Certificate)

A legal rating (A–G) of a building's energy efficiency. Required for all commercial property lettings in England and Wales.

M

Full article

MEES Compliance

Minimum Energy Efficiency Standards. UK law under SI 2015/962 requiring commercial properties to meet minimum EPC ratings, with proposed Band C by 2028.

P

PPN 002

PPN 002 (Procurement Policy Note 002)

UK government policy requiring a minimum 10% social value weighting on central government contract evaluations.

S

Regulation

SI 2015/962 Regulation 39

The statutory instrument that defines MEES penalty calculations using the rateable value formula. Fines range from £5,000 to £150,000.

T

Framework

TOMs Framework

Themes, Outcomes, and Measures. The standard framework for measuring social value in UK public sector procurement, using Oxford Social Value Bank proxy values.
