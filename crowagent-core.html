Platform features

Everything you need for MEES compliance

EPC Gap Analysis

Instant postcode lookup against the MHCLG EPC register. See your current band, the proposed Band C target, and the exact compliance gap, in seconds.

Retrofit Scenarios

Three costed upgrade pathways (Low Cost, Balanced, and Premium) each with itemised measures, estimated costs, and projected EPC improvement.

NPV Financial Modelling

Net present value calculations at the 3.5% HM Treasury Green Book discount rate with 2% Ofgem energy escalation. Investment-grade numbers for board packs.

Portfolio Dashboard

See every property's EPC band, compliance status, and penalty exposure in one view. Filter by risk level, sort by deadline urgency, and export to CSV.

PDF Compliance Reports

Branded, client-ready reports generated in under 30 seconds. Includes EPC analysis, penalty exposure, retrofit scenarios, and NPV, all citing SI 2015/962.

EPC Monitoring Alerts

Automatic alerts when an EPC certificate is updated or expires. Stay ahead of regulatory changes without manual tracking across your portfolio.
