Methodology & sources

Why you can trust this verdict

  • Both thresholds are required — >1,000 employees AND >€450M turnover — per Directive (EU) 2026/470 (Omnibus I, in force 18 March 2026).
  • The old 250-employee / €40M floor is superseded and is not used by this calculator.
  • Forward-risk status is triggered when you self-report a likelihood of crossing both thresholds within 2 reporting years.
  • Final scope depends on entity-level facts — parent vs subsidiary, listed-entity status, and Layer 3 value-chain exposure — which the full CSRD Checker covers.
