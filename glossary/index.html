Glossary

Compliance terms, defined.

Plain-English definitions of the regulations, frameworks and standards behind UK procurement, cyber security and sustainability reporting.

Term index

Procurement

PPN 002

Procurement Policy Note 002. Published February 2025 and mandatory from 1 October 2025, it sets a 10% minimum social value weighting on central-government tenders.

Read full definition Framework

TOMs

Themes, Outcomes and Measures. The standard framework for scoring social value in UK public procurement, using Oxford SVB proxy values.

Read full definition Directive

CSRD

Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive. Omnibus I reforms, subject to confirmation, would raise scope to firms over 1,000 staff and over €450M turnover.

Read full definition
Cyber security

Cyber Essentials

UK government-backed scheme covering five NCSC controls. Version 3.3 (Danzell) requires MFA on cloud admin from 27 April 2026.

Net zero

PPN 06/21

Procurement Policy Note 06/21. Requires a Carbon Reduction Plan from suppliers bidding for major government contracts.

Reporting

SECR

Streamlined Energy and Carbon Reporting. A UK framework for larger companies to report energy use and carbon emissions.

UK SDR

SDR

Sustainability Disclosure Requirements. The UK regime for sustainability reporting by companies and financial services firms.

Climate

TCFD

Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures. A global standard for reporting climate-related financial risk.

Standard

VSME

Voluntary reporting standard for SMEs. Published by EFRAG in 2024 as a simplified sustainability standard for smaller enterprises.
