Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Answers to common billing and product questions for UK SME owners, finance leads, and IT managers.

Is there a free tier for small users?

The CSRD Checker is free for all users and does not require an account. CrowAgent Core, CrowMark, CrowCyber, and CrowCash are paid products with a 14-day free trial. We also offer a range of free statutory tools including the MEES Risk Snapshot and PPN 002 Calculator for immediate assessment.

How does the 14-day trial work?

You get full access to the Pro tier of your chosen product for 14 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe to any plan (Starter, Pro, or Portfolio) or your account will revert to a read-only state until a subscription is active.

Are there any hidden setup fees?

No. Our pricing is transparent and all-inclusive. There are no setup fees, implementation charges, or training costs for our standard plans. Enterprise implementations may involve a one-time integration fee depending on requirements.

Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from the billing dashboard. Plan changes are pro-rated and applied immediately to your account access. If you upgrade, the new features are available instantly.

Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes. Enterprise plans start at £999/month and include SSO via SAML 2.0 (Okta, Entra ID), custom SLAs, dedicated customer success, and volume licensing for large property portfolios or multi-entity corporate groups.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. For annual Portfolio and Enterprise plans, we can also support payment via invoice and BACS transfer upon request to our finance team.
