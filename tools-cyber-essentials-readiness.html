How the readiness check works

From five questions to certification gap in three steps

FAQ

Common questions

What changed in v3.3?

Cyber Essentials v3.3 ('Danzell'), effective 28 April 2026, makes MFA gaps and 14-day patch SLA breaches auto-fails.

Does PPN 014 require Cyber Essentials?

PPN 014 mandates Cyber Essentials for in-scope government, NHS, and MOD suppliers.

Is this certification?

No. The readiness check is a self-test. Certification goes through an IASME-licensed Certifying Body.
